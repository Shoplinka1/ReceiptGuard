ReceiptGuard Landing Page Professional UX Audit

Do NOT redesign the website.

Do NOT change the color palette.

Do NOT change branding.

Do NOT change pricing.

Do NOT change backend logic.

Do NOT remove any existing features.

The goal is only to polish the landing page to make it look like a premium SaaS (Stripe, Notion, Linear, Vercel quality).

Before changing anything:
	•	Read the entire landing page.
	•	Read every section.
	•	Keep the current layout.
	•	Only improve wording, trust, hierarchy, spacing and conversion.

⸻

Section 1 — Hero

Current headline:

Your expenses, perfectly organized.

Current subheading:

ReceiptGuard automatically securely scans your Gmail to track receipts, monitor active subscriptions, and alert you before warranties expire or renewals hit.

Do NOT replace these.

Instead:

Improve the wording so it focuses more on the user’s outcome instead of features.

The visitor should immediately understand that ReceiptGuard helps them:
	•	never lose receipts
	•	never forget subscriptions
	•	never miss warranty deadlines
	•	organize purchases automatically

without making the headline longer.

✓ We never read personal conversations

✓ Disconnect anytime

✓ Your data stays encrypted

Do NOT exaggerate.

Product Vision

Receipt Guard is the digital home for everything people buy.

Users should be able to manage their entire purchase life cycle from one place.

Receipt Guard helps users organize:
	•	Purchases
	•	Receipts
	•	Invoices
	•	Product manuals
	•	Warranties
	•	Return windows
	•	Subscriptions
	•	Product documents

The app should protect users from losing money by reminding them about:
	•	subscription renewals
	•	warranty expirations
	•	return deadlines

The experience should feel premium, modern and effortless.

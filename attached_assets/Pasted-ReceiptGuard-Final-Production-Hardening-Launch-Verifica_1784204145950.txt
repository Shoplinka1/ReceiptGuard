	•	AED

	•	NOK
	•	DKK

Detect and store the actual completed transaction amount together with its correct currency.

Ignore informational monetary values including:
	•	exchange-rate examples
	•	“equivalent in…”
	•	previous balances
	•	estimated totals
	•	promotional prices
	•	“you saved…”
	•	taxes when a final charged total exists
	•	subtotals when a final total exists

Always prioritize the final charged amount.

If one email genuinely contains multiple completed purchases, extract each completed transaction separately.
